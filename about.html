The Path to Roboy Labs

Focusing on the intersection of human design and machine intelligence.

My journey in robotics didn't start with code, but with the practical realities of the manufacturing floor. Spending nearly a decade at **Seagate** taught me that even the most advanced hardware is limited if its software system is brittle.

As a Senior Research Engineer at **A*STAR**, I’ve focused on how we can make autonomous systems more resilient. I’ve spent the last several years exploring humanoid platforms and mobile manipulators, always asking the same question: *How can we make these machines easier to talk to and simpler to deploy?* [cite: 1, 2]

Roboy Labs is the result of that question. It is an experimental space where I apply 15 years of industrial experience in Singapore to build the frameworks of tomorrow.
